Resources

There are a lot of websites that I use frequently for different reasons. I despise shopping so retail websites like Amazon are great. Ars Technica is a favorite for technology and science related news stories. My favorite site for videogames is giantbomb.com which has a domain name that I'm always reluctant to share with others. Goodreads is a fantastic community for reading book reviews and keeping track of a reading list, and Netflix has almost replaced cable TV for me. The New York Times is my source for mainstream news though I usually just stick to the 'Most Read' articles.
