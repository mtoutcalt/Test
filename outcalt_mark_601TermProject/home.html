Harbor of Portsmouth, NH

Hello, my name is Mark and this is my Term Project for my 2nd class in the Computer Information Systems program at Boston University. I am pursuing the Web Application Development concentration and excited to learn new skills and to improve upon my current interests.

I'm currently located at the seacoast of New Hampshire but grew up in Wisconsin and have only lived in New England for 3 years. I love that this area has beaches, mountains, and is close-by to Boston, Portsmouth, NH, and Portland, ME.

I work for a large insurance company as a software developer. My technical background is mostly Java based and I have more experience developing backend services than front-end. I use Java EE frameworks on a daily basis such as Struts and Spring.
