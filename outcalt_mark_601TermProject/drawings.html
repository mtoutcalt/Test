Drawings

Here are some drawings that I've made in the last year. Most of them are from exercises in a workbook I've been following from the "Drawing With the Right Side of the Brain" series. This workbook has exercises that focus on countour, shape, negative space, line style, and other techniques that slowly build up your skills. I'm definitely not a professional but I do find it fun to see my drawings improve over time and I find the act of drawing to be relaxing. For anybody thinking to themselves that they are a "terrible artist" try to remember yourself as a kid before you put those labels and restrictions on yourself. I think with some practice anybody can become good at drawing.
