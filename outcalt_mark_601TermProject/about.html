About Mark Outcalt

Location

Currently living at the Seacoast of New Hampshire. Born and raised in WI, he moved to New England in 2012 after completing college in Milwaukee, WI. Moving from one cold climate to the next was not his smartest choice.

Technical Background

Graduated with a degree in Management Information Systems from the Milwaukee School of Engineering (MSOE).

For the last 3 years he has worked at a large insurance company as a Software Developer and works with server-side Java enterprise technologies.

He is currently pursuing a Masters in Computer Information Systems from Boston University.

Proficient in:

  • Java EE
  • SOAP Web Services
  • RESTful Web Services
  • Spring Framework
  • Struts
  • SQL
  • MongoDB
